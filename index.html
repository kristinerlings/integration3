The Sheng's Centuries-Old History

The Sheng, a traditional Chinese instrument, is said to have been created by the goddess Nü Wa, who is known as the creator of all humans.

Not only can it produce sound while breathing in and out, but it can also play multiple notes at once, making it a unique and versatile instrument.

The Sheng is made of bamboo sticks and has five main parts: the "sheng chest", "mouthpiece", "sheng pipes" which are placed in the chest with "finger-holes" and reeds placed on each pipe to produce individual notes.
